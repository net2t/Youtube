## [1.0.0] - 2025-01-01

### 🎉 Initial Release

#### Added
- ✂️ **Trim / Cut / Split** — trim by start/end time, split into fixed-length chunks (frame-based), remove middle sections
- 🔗 **Merge / Join** — concatenate multiple videos in custom order
- 📝 **Text & Subtitles** — add text overlays with position/timing control; burn-in SRT subtitle files
- 🖼️ **Logo / Watermark** — overlay PNG/JPG logos with position, size, and opacity settings
- 🎨 **Filters & Effects** — brightness, contrast, saturation, gamma sliders; grayscale, blur, flip H/V, vignette presets
- 🔊 **Audio Control** — volume adjustment, mute, remove audio, extract to MP3, replace audio track
- ⚡ **Speed Change** — speed up or slow down video (0.25x to 4x)
- 📐 **Crop & Resize** — presets for 1080p, 720p, Reels/TikTok, Square; custom crop; black-bar padding
- 🎬 **Intro / Outro** — attach intro and outro video clips to any video
- 📤 **Export Settings** — choose format (mp4/mkv/avi/mov/webm/gif), codec, CRF quality, FPS, resolution
- ⚙️ **Batch Convert** — convert all loaded files at once
- 🗂️ **File Sidebar** — load multiple files, view metadata (duration, resolution, FPS, size), remove files
- 📊 **Progress Bar** — real-time FFmpeg progress display
- 🌑 **Modern Dark UI** — built with CustomTkinter
